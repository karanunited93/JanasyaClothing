Strategic Roadmap

Three moves. One direction.

Scale immediately
Creator Programme
Creator content drives 8× more views at lower production cost than studio shoots. At current 2.8 activations/month, Janasya is leaving the biggest lever untouched.
  • 01Scale to 6–8 creator activations/month
  • 02Formalise @aartihiran — 3 posts/month minimum
  • 03Brief @thatwinkgirl for a second haul (Chikankari + Workwear)
  • 04Source 3–5 new micro-creators (30K–150K, ethnic fashion, India)
  • 05All creator briefs default to "Comment for link" CTA
Fix now
Content Calendar Rebalance
Chikankari = highest demand signal, fewest posts. Tunics = lowest performance, most posts. The calendar is running against the data on its highest-opportunity categories.
  • 01Chikankari → 2 posts/week minimum, always with "Comment for link"
  • 02Workwear → dedicated weekly series, SEO captions, consistent visual language
  • 03Cut tunic posts to max 2/month — reallocate bandwidth
  • 04Build Sep–Nov festive calendar (Navratri → Diwali → Weddings) by July
  • 05Test 19:00–21:00 IST posting window for 3 posts/month
Stop / change
Giveaways & Hashtag Strategy
Giveaways inflate comment metrics without building community. Generic hashtags (#ethnicwear 20M+ posts) offer zero discovery at 1.66L followers — the brand is invisible.
  • 01Max 1 giveaway/month — tied to product launches, not reach
  • 02Replace broad tags with medium-volume (50K–500K) category-specific tags
  • 03Stop #reels, #explore, #trending — pure noise, no discovery value
  • 04Build 4 owned hashtags (#YourJanasya, #DhoopChaavSS26, #EverydayChikankari, #WorkInJanasya)
  • 05Embed product SEO keywords in caption body on every product Reel
Growth Projection
If creator activations increase from 8 → 25/month and content rebalances toward Chikankari + Workwear, the projected reach uplift is 3–4× monthly video views.

At 1,66,232 followers with an 8× creator multiplier, 25 monthly creator posts alone would generate an estimated 14–18 lakh monthly video views — crossing the threshold for meaningful algorithm amplification and brand search lift on Myntra and Amazon.
